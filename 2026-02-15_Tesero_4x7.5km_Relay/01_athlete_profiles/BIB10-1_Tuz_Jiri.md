## Career Highlights
- **Olympic results:** First Olympic appearance at Milano Cortina 2026. **5th in Men's Sprint Classic** — extraordinary result for a 21-year-old in his Olympic debut. [source](https://www.proxcskiing.com/traditional-skiing/norwegian-superstar-powers-to-sprint-gold-at-milano-cortina-2026/)
- **World Championship results:** One participation (Trondheim 2025). 29th in sprint, 7th in team sprint classic (with Novak). [source](https://www.fis-ski.com/DB/general/athlete-biography.html?sectorcode=CC&competitorid=250564)
- **World Cup wins/podiums:** No podiums. Best result: **5th in Sprint Classic at Falun** (February 2025). [source](https://www.proxcskiing.com/traditional-skiing/jiri-tuz-a-rising-star-with-ambitious-goals/)
- **Junior World Championships:** Competed in Lyngen (NOR), Whistler (CAN), Planica (SLO). U23 debut in Schilpario (ITA) — 4th place in sprint. 1 x 7th. [source](https://www.proxcskiing.com/traditional-skiing/jiri-tuz-a-rising-star-with-ambitious-goals/)
- **European Youth Olympic Festival:** Silver medal in 7.5km classic at Vuokatti 2022. [source](https://www.proxcskiing.com/traditional-skiing/jiri-tuz-a-rising-star-with-ambitious-goals/)
- **First FIS event:** December 19, 2019 in St. Ulrich am Pillersee, Austria (127th in sprint). [source](https://www.the-sports.org/jiri-tuz-cross-country-skiing-spf716475.html)

## Key Storylines
- The breakout star of these Olympics from a smaller nation — 5th in the Olympic sprint classic at just 21 years old.
- Youngest member of any relay team in this race at 21.
- Works with Norwegian technique coach Ragnar Bragvin Andresen — cross-border coaching approach.
- Represents the next generation of Czech skiing alongside Matyáš Bauer.
- Classic sprint specialist assigned to Leg 1 (Classic) — perfectly suited role.
- His 4th-place qualification in the Olympic sprint was remarkable — ahead of many established names.
- First Czech skier to make an Olympic sprint final since Lukáš Bauer's era in distance racing.

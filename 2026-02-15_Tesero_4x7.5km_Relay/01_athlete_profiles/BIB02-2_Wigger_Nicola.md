# Nicola Wigger (SUI)
**Bib:** 2-2 | **FIS Code:** 3510644 | **DOB:** 2001-05-23 | **Age:** 24
**Club/Team:** Skiclub am Bachtel (Gibswil) | **National Team Tier:** B-team / National Group

## Personal Bio
- **Hometown / Place of Origin:** Gibswil, Zürcher Oberland (Zurich Highlands), Switzerland. Lives primarily in Davos during the winter season due to snow conditions. [source](https://nicolawigger.com/uber-mich/) [source](https://datasport.com/en/essentials/nicola-wigger-in-the-datasport-check/)
- **Family Background:** Part of a prominent Swiss cross-country skiing dynasty. Father **Jeremias Wigger** (born 1965) was one of Switzerland's best cross-country skiers in the 1980s and 1990s, competing at three Olympic Games — including a 4th-place finish in the 4x10km relay at the 1988 Calgary Olympics. Mother **Sylvia Wigger-Honegger** also competed at three Olympic Games and now serves as cross-country skiing chief (Langlauf-Chefin) at the Nordic Skiclub am Bachtel. Sister **Siri Wigger** (born 2003) is a two-time Youth Olympic champion (sprint and cross-sprint at 2020 Lausanne YOG) and Junior World Championship medalist. Father Jeremias still helps test equipment for his children. "In hardly any other Swiss family is cross-country skiing as present as in the Wigger family." [source](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jeremias_Wigger) [source](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Siri_Wigger) [source](https://fitforlife.ch/wp-content/uploads/attachments/Wigger-Familie_1.2020.pdf) [source](https://www.zo-online.ch/sport/2023-12-02/du-hoerst-sowieso-nicht-auf-mich-null)
- **Childhood & Introduction to Skiing:** His parents put him on cross-country skis for the first time at age two. By age six, he competed in his first race, which immediately fascinated him. He trained primarily at Skiclub am Bachtel in Gibswil, where his mother coached. Siri and Nicola both aspired to reach the Olympic Games, just like their parents. At age 16, the Olympic dream felt closer than it would in later years as he realized how difficult it is to break into the top 25. [source](https://datasport.com/en/essentials/nicola-wigger-in-the-datasport-check/) [source](https://www.zo-online.ch/sport/2023-12-02/du-hoerst-sowieso-nicht-auf-mich-null)

- **Personality / Public Persona:** Known for his authentic, transparent approach — one of the few athletes who openly documents his journey including the doubts and setbacks, not just the highlights. Describes himself as competition-oriented and focused, while integrating a sense of ease and fun to find balance. Sets himself the daily goal of expanding his comfort zone. His typical vlogging style shows him directly after races, breathing heavily in a sweat-soaked racing suit, hitting "record" for authentic in-the-moment reactions. Overcame a stress fracture (sesamoid bone) in autumn that threatened his season, reinventing his training with arm-powered machines and upper-body work. Making the Olympic team despite stricter selection criteria (reduced from 8 to 5 spots) and the injury made him "extremely proud." Works with coach Toni Livers.

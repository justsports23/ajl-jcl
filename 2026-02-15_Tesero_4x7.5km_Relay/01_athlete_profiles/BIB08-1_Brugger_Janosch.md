## Career Highlights
- **Olympic results:** Two-time Olympian (Beijing 2022, Milano Cortina 2026). Best finish: 5th in Men's 4x10km Relay at Beijing 2022. [source](https://olympics.com/en/athletes/janosch-brugger)
- **World Championship results:** Four World Championships participations. Bronze medal in Men's 4x10km Relay at Planica 2023. [source](https://olympics.com/en/athletes/janosch-brugger)
- **World Cup wins/podiums:** 1 x 3rd place (World Cup podium). [source](https://olympics.com/en/athletes/janosch-brugger)

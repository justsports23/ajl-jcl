# Florian Notz (GER)
**Bib:** 8-3 (Leg 3, Freestyle) | **FIS Code:** 3200376 | **DOB:** 24 April 1992 | **Age:** 33
**Club/Team:** SZ Römerstein / Zoll Ski Team | **National Team Tier:** A Team (2025/2026)

## Personal Bio
- **Hometown / Place of Origin:** Born in Bad Urach, grew up in Dettingen an der Erms (about 40 km south of Stuttgart), Swabian Alb. Lives and trains in Oberstdorf, Allgäu. [source](https://www.xc-ski.de/events/langlauf-weltcup/portraits/national/notz-florian-ger-portrait/)
- **Family Background:** Father Dieter Notz competed as a cross-country skier at the 1980 Olympic Games in Lake Placid and narrowly missed a medal with the relay team. Mother's side also contributed to his sporting genes. Younger brother Dominik is a fast long-distance and cross-country runner. As Florian said in 2014: *"Meine Gene habe ich von meinem Vater"* (I got my genes from my father). [source](https://www.xc-ski.de/events/langlauf-weltcup/portraits/national/notz-florian-ger-portrait/)
- **Childhood & Introduction to Skiing:** Has been skiing since he can remember. Joined SZ Römerstein at age eleven. Moved to the ski boarding school in Furtwangen in 2009. [source](https://www.xc-ski.de/events/langlauf-weltcup/portraits/national/notz-florian-ger-portrait/)
- **Partner / Relationship:** Previously lived with German cross-country skier Laura Gimmler in Oberstdorf. After the 2024/25 season, the couple separated. [source](https://www.xc-ski.de/events/langlauf-weltcup/portraits/national/notz-florian-ger-portrait/)
- **Children:** [DATA NOT FOUND]
- **Education:** Completed his Abitur (high school diploma) in 2011 at the ski boarding school in Furtwangen. Joined the Zoll Ski Team afterward. [source](https://www.xc-ski.de/events/langlauf-weltcup/portraits/national/notz-florian-ger-portrait/)
- **Off-Snow Interests:** Sports, listening to music, hanging out with friends. Favorite food: his mom's Mousse au Chocolat and pasta. [source](https://www.xc-ski.de/events/langlauf-weltcup/portraits/national/notz-florian-ger-portrait/)
- **Physical Stats:** 187 cm tall, 70 kg. [source](https://www.xc-ski.de/events/langlauf-weltcup/portraits/national/notz-florian-ger-portrait/)
- **Notable Quotes:**
  - *"In der Ruhe liegt die Kraft"* (Strength lies in calmness) — life motto. [source](https://www.xc-ski.de/events/langlauf-weltcup/portraits/national/notz-florian-ger-portrait/)
  - *"Since my fitness is already good, that wasn't the focus this time. My main goal was to consciously train at a relaxed pace."* — ahead of 2025/26 Tour de Ski. [source](https://www.proxcskiing.com/traditional-skiing/german-national-team-for-tour-de-ski-2025-2026/)
- **Personality / Public Persona:** Known as a calm, methodical competitor. Illness at critical moments has repeatedly hampered his career — he was never part of the German relay team when they reached the podium (including Planica 2023 bronze). A podium finish in individual races has eluded him. [source](https://www.xc-ski.de/events/langlauf-weltcup/portraits/national/notz-florian-ger-portrait/)

## Career Highlights
- **Olympic results:** Two-time Olympian (Beijing 2022, Milano Cortina 2026). Best finish: 5th in Men's 4x10km Relay at Beijing 2022. [source](https://www.olympics.com/en/athletes/florian-notz)
- **World Championship results:** Five World Championships participations. Best results: 2 x 6th place. Was NOT on the relay team for the Planica 2023 bronze due to illness. [source](https://www.xc-ski.de/events/langlauf-weltcup/portraits/national/notz-florian-ger-portrait/)
- **World Cup wins/podiums:** No podiums. Best results: 3 x 4th place. Best individual finishes include 7th in Lahti and 8th in the 50km freestyle mass start at Holmenkollen. [source](https://www.xc-ski.de/events/langlauf-weltcup/portraits/national/notz-florian-ger-portrait/)
- **U23 World Championships:** U23 World Champion in freestyle individual start. [source](https://www.xc-ski.de/events/langlauf-weltcup/portraits/national/notz-florian-ger-portrait/)
- **Junior World Championships:** Top ten individually; 4th with relay team. [source](https://www.xc-ski.de/events/langlauf-weltcup/portraits/national/notz-florian-ger-portrait/)

## Key Storylines
- At 33, the most experienced member of the German relay team and the elder statesman of the squad.
- Father Dieter Notz was an Olympian in 1980 — second-generation Olympic skier.
- Has never been part of a relay team that reached the podium at Worlds or Olympics despite being consistently on the national team.
- Separated from long-time partner Laura Gimmler after the 2024/25 season — personal turbulence.
- Season form is solid in freestyle distance events (12th in Davos, 14th in Trondheim) — good signs for a freestyle relay leg.
- Illness has been a recurring issue at major championships throughout his career.

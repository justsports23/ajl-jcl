## Career Highlights
- **Olympic Results:**

  - 2026 Milano Cortina: **8th** in Skiathlon 10+10km; 41st in 10km Free [source](https://www.olympics.com/en/milano-cortina-2026/results/athlete-details/22211)

  - 2025 Trondheim: **4th** in Team Sprint Classic (with Pellegrino); Skiathlon — finished; Relay — 6th [source](https://www.nordicmag.info/en/cross-country-skiing-trondheim-my-rivals-were-stronger-than-me-admits-federico-pellegrino-fourth-in-the-sprint-team-with-davide-graz-0305/)

## Technique & Format Profile
- **Classic strength:** Strong and improving — 10th in 10km classic at Oberhof (Jan 2026); multiple Italian Championship titles in classic. Described as wanting to prove himself in classic. [source](https://www.fondoitalia.it/2026/01/18/sci-di-fondo-davide-graz-celebra-la-top-ten-nella-10-chilometri-di-oberhof-volevo-dar-un-bel-segnale-anche-in-tecnica-classica/)
- **Freestyle strength:** Capable — consistent results in freestyle events; 8th in Olympic skiathlon (includes freestyle leg)
- **Sprint ability:** Developing — reached first-ever World Cup sprint semifinal at Davos (Dec 2025); historically strong at junior level. [source](https://www.fondoitalia.it/2025/12/13/sci-di-fondo-davide-graz-dopo-la-prima-semifinale-in-una-sprint-un-grande-traguardo-anche-se-ce-un-po-di-amaro-in-bocca/)
- **Distance preference:** Versatile — described as "eclectic" talent; performs in both sprint and distance events
- **Best format:** Skiathlon and relay — 8th in Olympic skiathlon, 8th in Olympic relay (2022)
- **Climbing ability:** Strong — raised at 1,200m altitude in Sappada; accustomed to mountain training

## Key Storylines
- **Olympic Skiathlon Breakout:** His 8th-place finish in the Olympic skiathlon at Milano Cortina was a career-best result at a major championship individual event, establishing him as a genuine top-10 competitor.
- **Classic Leg Assignment:** Selected as the opening classic leg in the relay — his Oberhof 10km classic top-10 (10th) and Italian Championship classic titles validate this selection.
- **Home Olympics:** As part of the Italian team racing on home snow, Graz brings the crowd factor. Born in the Dolomites (Pieve di Cadore/Sappada), this is essentially his home region.
- **Two-time Olympian:** Beijing 2022 experience gives him relay expertise, having anchored Italy to 8th in the 4x10km there.
- **Rising Form:** Trajectory has been upward all season, from mid-pack Tour de Ski finishes to top-10 results at Oberhof and the Olympic skiathlon.
- **Pre-season dominance:** Won a 10km cross-country running race at Oberhof in September 2025, beating Pellegrino and Barp, signaling excellent fitness. [source](https://www.fondoitalia.it/2025/09/26/sci-di-fondo-davide-graz-vince-la-10-km-di-corsa-campestre-a-oberhof-mettendosi-alle-spalle-pellegrino-e-barp/)

# Hugo Lapalus (FRA)
**Bib:** 4-2 | **FIS Code:** 3190529 | **DOB:** 1998-07-09 | **Age:** 27
**Club/Team:** C.S. La Clusaz / Armées-Emhm | **National Team Tier:** A-team

## Personal Bio
- **Hometown / Place of Origin:** Manigod, Haute-Savoie, France. Member of C.S. La Clusaz for 12+ years. [source](https://www.laclusaz.com/en/le-mag/portrait-en/hugo-lapalus-champion-un-job-a-plein-temps/)
- **Family Background:** Not from a particularly sporting family. His parents were outdoor enthusiasts who would take the family hiking and exploring the mountains around La Clusaz. He has a younger sister who was also in the La Clusaz sports club doing alpine skiing, but she stopped. In his own words: *"Les parents nous emmenaient nous balader. Quand tu nais au pied des pistes, forcément tu mets les skis très tôt."* ("Our parents took us out walking. When you're born at the foot of the slopes, you naturally start skiing very early.") [source](https://www.laclusaz.com/en/le-mag/no-story-no-future-en/les-mousquetaires-du-fond/)
- **Childhood & Introduction to Skiing:** Born at the foot of the ski slopes in the La Clusaz area, he naturally started skiing at a very young age. Initially tried alpine skiing but it did not suit him. Switched to cross-country skiing for fun and immediately took to the atmosphere and spirit of the discipline, eventually pursuing it to the highest level. [source](https://www.laclusaz.com/en/le-mag/no-story-no-future-en/les-mousquetaires-du-fond/)

- **Personality / Public Persona:** A quiet, hardworking mountain man from Manigod who is deeply loyal to the La Clusaz skiing community. Described as humble and grounded. Emotional about results — he broke down in tears during a Eurosport interview after a disappointing race at the 2026 Olympics, showing how deeply he cares. Compared by some to former La Clusaz champion Vincent Vittoz. Dedicates 11 months per year to training and competition. Coaches and teammates view him as a model of consistent improvement.

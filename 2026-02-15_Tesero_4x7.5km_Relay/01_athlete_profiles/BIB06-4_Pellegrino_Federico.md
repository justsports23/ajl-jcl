## Key Storylines
- **Farewell performance?** Likely his final Olympics at age 35 — wants to end on a high at home
- **Italian national hero** — 2 Olympic silver medals, 62 WC podiums, 21 WC wins
- **Home crowd factor** — Racing in front of 15,000+ Italian fans at Tesero will be electric

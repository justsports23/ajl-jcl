# Matyáš Bauer (CZE)
**Bib:** 10-3 (Leg 3, Freestyle) | **FIS Code:** 3150911 | **DOB:** 12 April 2004 | **Age:** 21
**Club/Team:** LK Slovan Karlovy Vary | **National Team Tier:** A Team (2025/2026 — first year with senior national team)

## Personal Bio
- **Hometown / Place of Origin:** Born in Ostrov, Czech Republic. Lives with his parents in Boží Dar (a mountain village in the Krušné Hory / Ore Mountains). [source](https://www.proxcskiing.com/traditional-skiing/matyas-bauer-my-dad-is-my-biggest-inspiration/)
- **Family Background:** Son of the legendary Czech cross-country skier **Lukáš Bauer** (born 18 August 1977) — who won Olympic silver in 15km classic (Turin 2006), Olympic bronze in 15km freestyle (Vancouver 2010), was the overall winner of the Tour de Ski and FIS Cross-Country World Cup in 2007-08, and won the Tour de Ski again in 2010. Mother is **Kateřina Balatková**, a former cross-country skier. [source](https://www.proxcskiing.com/traditional-skiing/matyas-bauer-my-dad-is-my-biggest-inspiration/) [source](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Luk%C3%A1%C5%A1_Bauer)
- **Childhood & Introduction to Skiing:** Grew up in Boží Dar, a mountain village, with parents who were both skiers. Was involved in both skiing and football but ultimately chose cross-country skiing — his parents' sport. [source](https://www.proxcskiing.com/traditional-skiing/matyas-bauer-my-dad-is-my-biggest-inspiration/)

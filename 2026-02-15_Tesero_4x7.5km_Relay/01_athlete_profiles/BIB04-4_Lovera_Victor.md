# Victor Lovera (FRA)
**Bib:** 4-4 | **FIS Code:** 3190580 | **DOB:** 2000-06-05 | **Age:** 25
**Club/Team:** Team Vercors Isère / Ski Nordique Chartreuse | **National Team Tier:** A-team (Group A since spring 2025)

## Personal Bio
- **Hometown / Place of Origin:** Le Sappey-en-Chartreuse, Isère, France (Chartreuse massif near Grenoble) [source](http://fondation-grenoble-inp.fr/en/nos-actions/victor-lovera/) [source](https://www.nordicmag.info/en/cross-country-skiing-les-rousses-i-knew-i-could-do-it-says-victor-lovera-best-frenchman-in-the-individual-event-0117/)
- **Family Background:** [DATA NOT FOUND] — No detailed public information about parents or siblings.
- **Childhood & Introduction to Skiing:** Started cross-country skiing at age 6 at Ski Nordique Chartrousin in Le Sappey-en-Chartreuse. Initially, competitions were very difficult for him and he struggled. At age 15, he committed fully to the sport, joining the Pole Espoir at the Lycee Sport-Etudes in Villard-de-Lans. Became French Junior Champion in 2017 at just 17. A serious back injury in 2021 disrupted his career, but he rebuilt his form through Team Vercors Isère. [source](http://fondation-grenoble-inp.fr/en/nos-actions/victor-lovera/) [source](https://www.grenoble-inp.fr/fr/vie-etudiante/sport-de-haut-niveau-objectif-jo-pour-victor-lovera)
- **Partner / Relationship:** [DATA NOT FOUND]
- **Children:** [DATA NOT FOUND]
- **Education:** Engineering student at Grenoble INP - Ensimag (Universite Grenoble Alpes), specialising in financial engineering. Completed a bachelor's degree in mathematics and computer science from UGA, then entered the Ensimag engineering program. He has extended his 2nd year over two years thanks to his high-level athlete (SHN) status, allowing him to balance studies with World Cup racing. Recipient of a Fondation Grenoble INP scholarship. He is the only athlete in his training group who also pursues university studies. [source](https://www.grenoble-inp.fr/fr/vie-etudiante/sport-de-haut-niveau-objectif-jo-pour-victor-lovera) [source](http://fondation-grenoble-inp.fr/en/nos-actions/victor-lovera/)
- **Off-Snow Interests:** Mathematics and computer science. His intellectual rigour and analytical approach are often cited as contributing to his racing strategy. [source](https://www.equipedefrance.com/athlete/victor-lovera)

  - *"One hell of a strong race."* — reflecting on his 15th place Olympic skiathlon debut at Tesero [source](https://www.nordicmag.info/en/2026-olympics-cross-country-skiing-one-hell-of-a-strong-race-victor-lovera-15th-in-the-skiathlon-looks-back-on-his-first-games-for-nordic-magazine-0209/)
  - *"Taking part in this competition [World Championships] is the ultimate achievement."* — on his first senior Worlds selection in 2025 [source](https://www.nordicmag.info/en/cross-country-skiing-taking-part-in-this-competition-is-the-ultimate-achievement-after-his-fine-performances-in-the-world-cup-victor-lovera-will-be-competing-in-his-very-first-senior-world-ch-0219/)
- **Personality / Public Persona:** A rare dual-path athlete who combines elite-level World Cup skiing with engineering studies at one of France's top engineering schools. Known for intellectual rigour, resilience (overcame a serious back injury in 2021), and a long-term, methodical approach to performance. Described by Equipe de France as having "l'exigence intellectuelle au service du ski" (intellectual rigour at the service of skiing). Won the Transjurassienne 2025 and Foulee Blanche 2024 marathon races, showing his endurance capabilities. Was dropped from the national team structure in spring 2024 but fought his way back through strong Team Vercors Isère results to earn Group A selection and an Olympic spot. [source](https://www.equipedefrance.com/athlete/victor-lovera) [source](https://www.grenoble-inp.fr/fr/vie-etudiante/sport-de-haut-niveau-objectif-jo-pour-victor-lovera) [source](https://www.nordicmag.info/en/cross-country-skiing-whats-next-looks-good-and-i-cant-wait-to-change-dimension-back-in-the-french-senior-team-victor-lovera-talks-to-nordic-magazine-0513/)

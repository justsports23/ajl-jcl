# Martino Carollo (ITA)
**Bib:** 6-3 | **FIS Code:** 3290724 | **DOB:** 2003-06-19 | **Age:** 22
**Club/Team:** G.S. Fiamme Oro (Italian State Police) | **National Team Tier:** A-team
**Coach:** Markus Cramer (national team head coach)

## Personal Bio
- **Hometown / Place of Origin:** Borgo San Dalmazzo, province of Cuneo, Piedmont, Italy. [source](https://milanocortina2026.coni.it/en/italia-team/team/scheda_atleta/2674:Martino_CAROLLO.html)
- **Family Background:** Comes from a family of sportspeople. Has a younger brother, Michele Carollo (born 2005), who competes in biathlon for Centro Sportivo Carabinieri and is on Italy's Junior National biathlon team. Both brothers grew up in the Sci Club Entracque Alpi Marittime, a renowned talent factory for cross-country and biathlon in the Maritime Alps. Michele studies Sports Science (Scienze Motorie) at university. [source](https://www.cuneodice.it/varie/cuneo-e-valli/borgo-san-dalmazzo-celebra-i-suoi-campioni-del-biathlon_107851.html) [source](https://www.targatocn.it/2025/10/08/leggi-notizia/argomenti/sport-2/articolo/borgo-san-dalmazzo-fa-il-tifo-per-martino-carollo-in-vista-di-milano-cortina-2026.html)
- **Childhood & Introduction to Skiing:** Parents put skis on his feet at age three. He also took up mountain biking and, fascinated by shooting, tried biathlon (the discipline his brother chose). Ultimately chose cross-country skiing because of "the fewer unknowns and the way a race can be managed." Left home in Borgo San Dalmazzo as a teenager to move to Val di Fiemme, where the Fiamme Oro offered him a training spot alongside veterans like Dietmar Nockler and Simone Dapra under coach Marco Selle. [source](https://milanocortina2026.coni.it/en/italia-team/team/scheda_atleta/2674:Martino_CAROLLO.html) [source](https://www.fondoitalia.it/2023/04/17/sci-di-fondo-conosciamo-martino-carollo-vincitore-dellopa-cup-junior-ho-lasciato-casa-per-il-fondo-ma-non-e-un-sacrificio-perche-e-qualcosa-che-amo-e-in-cui-credo/)

- **Personality / Public Persona:** Humble, dedicated young talent who embodies the values of hard work, humility, and tenacity. At 22, is the youngest member of the Italian relay squad. Left home as a teenager to pursue his dream but doesn't view it as sacrifice. Known for his quiet determination and strong family bonds. The entire town of Borgo San Dalmazzo rallied behind him ahead of the Olympics. [source](https://www.targatocn.it/2025/10/08/leggi-notizia/argomenti/sport-2/articolo/borgo-san-dalmazzo-fa-il-tifo-per-martino-carollo-in-vista-di-milano-cortina-2026.html) [source](https://www.fondoitalia.it/2023/04/17/sci-di-fondo-conosciamo-martino-carollo-vincitore-dellopa-cup-junior-ho-lasciato-casa-per-il-fondo-ma-non-e-un-sacrificio-perche-e-qualcosa-che-amo-e-in-cui-credo/)

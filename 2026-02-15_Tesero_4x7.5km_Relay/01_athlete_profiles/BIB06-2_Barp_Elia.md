# Elia Barp (ITA)
**Bib:** 6-2 | **FIS Code:** 3290612 | **DOB:** 2002-12-19 | **Age:** 23
**Club/Team:** G.S. Fiamme Gialle | **National Team Tier:** A-team

## Personal Bio
- **Hometown / Place of Origin:** Born in Belluno; originally from Trichiana di Borgo Valbelluna, Veneto, Italy. Moved to Falcade to be closer to training facilities. [source](https://it.wikipedia.org/wiki/Elia_Barp) [source](https://www.rivistaundici.com/2025/12/31/elia-barp-milano-cortina-2026/)
- **Family Background:** Father Beppi, mother Barbara, and younger brother Dante (also a cross-country skier). Uncle Toni is a long-distance skier and captain of the Vasabroke Poiana team. Elia is the grandson of one of the founders of the "Gruppo Fondisti Trichiana" -- cross-country skiing has always been a family affair. [source](https://www.ilnordest.it/dossier/olimpiadi-milano-cortina-2026/elia-barp-debutto-olimpiadi-sci-fondo-nr9ki8rd) [source](https://ilveses.com/elia-barp-un-ragazzo-doro/)
- **Childhood & Introduction to Skiing:** Learned to ski with the Gruppo Sportivo Castionese local club, then grew up through the Ski College Veneto in Falcade. His family eventually moved from Trichiana to Falcade to support his training. Attended the Liceo Scientifico Sportivo (sports-focused high school) in Agordo. Watched Federico Pellegrino win the 2017 World Championship sprint on TV as a teenager, and Pellegrino became his idol. [source](https://www.ilnordest.it/dossier/olimpiadi-milano-cortina-2026/elia-barp-debutto-olimpiadi-sci-fondo-nr9ki8rd) [source](https://www.rivistaundici.com/2025/12/31/elia-barp-milano-cortina-2026/) [source](https://fondazionecortina.com/en/team/elia-barp/)
- **Partner / Relationship:** Girlfriend Virginia Cena, who is also a cross-country skier. [source](https://www.ilnordest.it/dossier/olimpiadi-milano-cortina-2026/elia-barp-debutto-olimpiadi-sci-fondo-nr9ki8rd)
- **Children:** No public information.
- **Education:** Attended the Liceo Scientifico Sportivo di Agordo. Part of the Ski College Veneto and Fondazione Cortina Youth Project. [source](https://www.ilnordest.it/dossier/olimpiadi-milano-cortina-2026/elia-barp-debutto-olimpiadi-sci-fondo-nr9ki8rd) [source](https://fondazionecortina.com/en/team/elia-barp/)

- **Health:** Suffered a cardiac arrhythmia during the 2025 World Championships in Trondheim, requiring a minor surgical procedure. Successfully recovered and returned to full competition. [source](https://www.olympics.com/it/milano-cortina-2026/notizie/elia-barp-milano-cortina-2026-vogliamo-medaglia-sci-fondo-intervista/) [source](https://www.rivistaundici.com/2025/12/31/elia-barp-milano-cortina-2026/)
- **Personality / Public Persona:** Considered the leader of the new generation of Italian cross-country skiing. Quiet determination combined with rapidly growing confidence. Deeply inspired by Federico Pellegrino and sees helping Pellegrino achieve a team medal at his farewell Olympics as a personal mission. Known for steady, consistent improvement season after season. [source](https://www.olympics.com/it/milano-cortina-2026/notizie/elia-barp-milano-cortina-2026-vogliamo-medaglia-sci-fondo-intervista/) [source](https://www.rivistaundici.com/2025/12/31/elia-barp-milano-cortina-2026/)

# Antoine Cyr (CAN)
**Bib:** 5-2 | **FIS Code:** 3100409 | **DOB:** 1998-09-18 | **Age:** 27
**Club/Team:** Centre National Pierre-Harvey / Club Skinouk (Gatineau) | **National Team Tier:** A-team

## Personal Bio
- **Hometown / Place of Origin:** Gatineau, Quebec, Canada [source](https://olympic.ca/team-canada/antoine-cyr/) [source](https://nordiqcanada.ca/athlete/antoine-cyr/)
- **Family Background:** Both parents were cross-country skiers and outdoor enthusiasts. His father moved from Montreal to Gatineau specifically for the cross-country skiing lifestyle. His mother was born and raised in Gatineau. They introduced him to skiing from a very young age and passed on their passion through family excursions in Gatineau Park. [source](https://olympic.ca/2024/10/22/i-have-a-super-competitive-personality-cross-country-skier-antoine-cyr-has-big-goals-for-2024-25-and-beyond/) [source](https://gatineauloppet.com/news-en/antoine-cyr-named-co-honorary-chair/?lang=en)
- **Childhood & Introduction to Skiing:** Grew up just 400 metres from Gatineau Park, one of Quebec's premier cross-country skiing areas. Started skiing as early as he can remember, around age 2. Participated in his first Gatineau Loppet at age 2 and entered competitive racing at age 10. Developed through Club Skinouk, a smaller club that taught him the value of great teammates and coaching. Left home at age 18 to join the national training centre. *"I'm super lucky because I grew up in the Ottawa Valley... we are super fortunate to have Gatineau Park, which is an unreal place to learn to cross country ski — it was 400 metres from my door!"* [source](https://olympic.ca/2024/10/22/i-have-a-super-competitive-personality-cross-country-skier-antoine-cyr-has-big-goals-for-2024-25-and-beyond/) [source](https://gatineauloppet.com/news-en/antoine-cyr-named-co-honorary-chair/?lang=en)
- **Partner / Relationship:** [DATA NOT FOUND]
- **Children:** [DATA NOT FOUND]
- **Education:** [DATA NOT FOUND] — Left home at 18 to join the national training centre full-time.
- **Off-Snow Interests:** Very passionate about cycling (mountain biking, road cycling, cyclocross). Works as a mechanic at Boreal Bike Shop. Enjoys camping. [source](https://nordiqcanada.ca/athlete/antoine-cyr/)
- **Notable Quotes:**
  - *"I have a super competitive personality."* — on his drive [source](https://olympic.ca/2024/10/22/i-have-a-super-competitive-personality-cross-country-skier-antoine-cyr-has-big-goals-for-2024-25-and-beyond/)
  - *"The Gatineau Loppet is an event that holds a special place in my heart. It's during this unifying event, located a few minutes from the family home, that I took my first steps in cross-country skiing."* [source](https://gatineauloppet.com/news-en/antoine-cyr-named-co-honorary-chair/?lang=en)
  - *"If you have fun you will go fast."* — his favourite motto [source](https://nordiqcanada.ca/athlete/antoine-cyr/)
- **Personality / Public Persona:** Bilingual (French/English) with a fiercely competitive spirit and a deep connection to the Gatineau skiing community. Named co-honorary chair of the Gatineau Loppet. Inspired by Alex Harvey, whom he had the opportunity to train alongside. Known for versatility across sprint and distance events and both techniques. Finished 10th in the 2023-24 World Cup overall standings — Canada's best individual ranking. Also an avid cyclist who works part-time as a bike mechanic. [source](https://olympic.ca/2024/10/22/i-have-a-super-competitive-personality-cross-country-skier-antoine-cyr-has-big-goals-for-2024-25-and-beyond/) [source](https://nordiqcanada.ca/athlete/antoine-cyr/) [source](https://gatineauloppet.com/news-en/antoine-cyr-named-co-honorary-chair/?lang=en)

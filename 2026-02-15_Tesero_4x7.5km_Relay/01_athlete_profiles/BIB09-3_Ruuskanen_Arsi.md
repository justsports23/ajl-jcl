## Key Storylines
- Olympic debut at age 26 — late bloomer reaching his first Games.
- 11th in the Olympic skiathlon was a strong debut performance, beating many more experienced skiers.
- U23 World Champion in classic (2022) — foundational achievement.
- Part of Finland's A-Team alongside Niskanen, Vuorinen — trusted by the Finnish federation for the relay.
- Assigned to Leg 3 (Freestyle) despite being stronger in classic — suggests Finnish team values his overall distance ability and steady pace.
- Selected for the Olympics in a second round of nominations, alongside Johanna Matintalo and Eero Hirvonen.

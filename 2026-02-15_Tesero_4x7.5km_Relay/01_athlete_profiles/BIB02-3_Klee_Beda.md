## Personal Bio
- **Hometown / Place of Origin:** Wattwil, Toggenburg, Canton of St. Gallen, Switzerland. Represents Skiclub Speer Ebnat-Kappel. The Toggenburg is a valley region in eastern Switzerland known for its Alpine culture and winter sports tradition. [source](https://www.linkedin.com/in/beda-klee-969829315/) [source](https://www.eqs-news.com/news/corporate/professional-cross-country-skier-beda-klee-is-the-new-brand-ambassador-for-valoras-own-brand-ok/25f4581b-8499-4cee-9e03-a4de7a434ea6_en)
- **Family Background:** From the Toggenburg valley in eastern Switzerland. No detailed family information is publicly available. [DATA NOT FOUND for parents/siblings]
- **Childhood & Introduction to Skiing:** Grew up in Wattwil in the Toggenburg region. Attended a sports grammar school (Sportgymnasium), commuting twice a week by train from Wattwil to Davos. Looks back on his school days with nostalgia — recalled treating himself to a hot dog at the "avec" convenience store in Landquart when changing trains. Began competing internationally in U18 Alpen Cup races from 2013. At the 2013 European Youth Olympic Winter Festival in Predeal (Romania), he placed 25th in the 7.5km classic. By 2016, he was 4th in the 10km classic at Junior Worlds in Rasnov. Made his World Cup debut in Davos in December 2017. ## Career Highlights
- **Olympic Results:** 2026 Milano Cortina: 10km Free — 21st (first Olympics)
- **World Championship Results:**
  - 4 World Championships participations (2019, 2021, 2023, 2025)
  - Best result: 5th in Men's 4x10km Relay in 2021
- **World Cup:** 7 top-ten finishes in one season (2023/24); 13th in overall WC ranking; 1 podium — 1 x 2nd. Switzerland's best long-distance skier.
- **Tour de Ski:** 5th overall (2023/24) — breakthrough result
- **Alpen Cup:** Won first Alpen Cup race in 15km freestyle mass start in St. Ulrich am Pillersee (2017/18)
- **Junior World Championships:** 4th in 10km classic at 2016 Rasnov; 6th and 9th in relays

## Technique & Format Profile
- **Classic strength:** Strong — developed through years of distance racing
- **Freestyle strength:** Strong — selected for freestyle leg 3
- **Sprint ability:** Not a sprint specialist

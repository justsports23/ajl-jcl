# Théo Schely (FRA)
**Bib:** 4-1 | **FIS Code:** 3190528 | **DOB:** 1999-09-06 | **Age:** 26
**Club/Team:** C.S. La Clusaz / Police Nationale | **National Team Tier:** A-team

## Personal Bio
- **Hometown / Place of Origin:** Manigod, Haute-Savoie, France (born in Annecy) [source](https://www.ski-nordique.net/ski-es-tu-theo-schely.6323729-358825.html)
- **Family Background:** No detailed public information about his parents. He describes his La Clusaz team group as "une famille" (a family), crediting team cohesion for performance. [source](https://www.nordicmag.info/ski-de-fond-il-va-falloir-que-je-retrouve-cette-confiance-en-passant-des-caps-les-confidences-de-theo-schely-pour-nordic-magazine-1107/)
- **Childhood & Introduction to Skiing:** Grew up in Manigod near La Clusaz. Initially tried other sports but results were not impressive; switched to cross-country skiing at age 10 at the C.S. La Clusaz club. *"Les résultats n'étaient pas flamboyants et j'avais envie de changement, ce qui m'a amené au ski de fond à l'âge de 10 ans au club de La Clusaz."* [source](https://www.ski-nordique.net/ski-es-tu-theo-schely.6323729-358825.html)
- **Partner / Relationship:** In a relationship with Norwegian cross-country skier and Ski Classics star Magni Smedas (born 1995, from Dalsbygda, Norway). They began dating in early 2025 after Schely threw his sunglasses into the crowd at the Tour de Ski and Smedas caught them — she reached out via Instagram to return them, sparking a long-distance romance. They have been living together in Lillehammer, Norway. [source](https://www.proxcskiing.com/long-distance/smedas-missed-the-season-but-found-love/)
- **Children:** No public information.
- **Education:** Member of the French National Police (Police Nationale) winter sports team since 2023. Completed police training in Grenoble and Annecy in spring 2023 through the "Mission Sport" program. [source](https://www.nordicmag.info/en/xc-skiing-injury-goals-new-recruit-of-the-national-police-theo-schely-talks-about-his-summer-preparation-0823/) [source](https://www.nordicmag.info/en/biathlon-cross-country-skiing-jeanne-richard-mathis-desloges-and-theo-schely-received-at-the-national-police-headquarters-0518/)
- **Off-Snow Interests:** [DATA NOT FOUND]
- **Notable Quotes:**
  - *"Il suffira d'une bonne journée à La Clusaz et toute ma carrière sera réussie."* ("One good day in La Clusaz and my whole career will be a success.") — on the 2030 Olympics bid for La Clusaz [source](https://www.nordicmag.info/ski-de-fond-il-va-falloir-que-je-retrouve-cette-confiance-en-passant-des-caps-les-confidences-de-theo-schely-pour-nordic-magazine-1107/)
  - *"Magni is the more structured one, while I take things as they come."* — on his relationship with Magni Smedas [source](https://www.proxcskiing.com/long-distance/smedas-missed-the-season-but-found-love/)
  - *"Il va falloir que je retrouve cette confiance en passant des caps."* ("I need to regain that confidence by passing milestones.") — on his preparation for the Olympic season [source](https://www.nordicmag.info/ski-de-fond-il-va-falloir-que-je-retrouve-cette-confiance-en-passant-des-caps-les-confidences-de-theo-schely-pour-nordic-magazine-1107/)
- **Personality / Public Persona:** Described as easy-going, sociable, and team-oriented. Aspires to be an "all-round" skier capable of competing in every format. Trains closely with Hugo Lapalus and Jules Chappaz at La Clusaz and is known for a laid-back personality — his girlfriend describes him as spontaneous while she is the more structured one. A member of the Police Nationale sports team alongside teammates Mathis Desloges and biathletes.

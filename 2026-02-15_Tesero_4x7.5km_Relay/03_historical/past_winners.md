The 2026 Milano Cortina Olympics is the first Games to contest the men's relay over 4 x 7.5 km (gender-equal distances). [Source: Olympics.com](https://www.olympics.com/en/milano-cortina-2026/sports/cross-country-skiing)

## Key Observations
- **Norway** has dominated the men's relay in recent years, winning World Championship gold every edition from 2011–2025 (except 2013 and 2017 when Russia won)
- **France** has been remarkably consistent, earning Olympic relay medals in 2014, 2018, and 2022
- **Italy** won Olympic relay gold at the **2006 Turin Games** — the last time the Olympics were held in Italy
- **Sweden** has multiple Olympic golds (2010, 2014) and is a perennial contender
- **Switzerland** emerged as a surprise silver medalist at the 2025 Worlds in Trondheim

# Sora Yachi (JPN)
**Bib:** 12 | **FIS Code:** 211028 | **DOB:** May 4, 2000 | **Age:** 25
**Club/Team:** JAL (Japan Airlines) — Athlete Employee | **National Team Tier:** Japan A-team

## Personal Bio
- **Hometown / Place of Origin:** Born in **Shiwa-chō (紫波町)**, Iwate Prefecture, Japan — a small rural town in the Tōhoku region. Height 168cm. [source](https://www.fis-ski.com/DB/general/athlete-biography.html?sectorcode=nk&competitorid=211028&type=result) [source](https://yachisora.com/2021/10/01/%E8%B0%B7%E5%9C%B0%E3%80%80%E5%AE%99/)
- **Family Background:** [DATA NOT FOUND]
- **Childhood & Introduction to Skiing:** As an elementary school student, he was a *"normal sports-loving kid"* who played **baseball** (1 year), **soccer**, and **alpine skiing** (3 years). He was recruited through the **Iwate Super Kids Discovery and Development Project** (いわてスーパーキッズ発掘育成事業, 5th cohort) — a regional talent identification program designed to find and develop world-class athletes from Iwate Prefecture. Attended a lecture by Sangeida Reiichi where the phrase *"People can walk the life they envision"* struck him deeply and inspired him to pursue elite sport. Switched to ski jumping in middle school, then transitioned into Nordic Combined. Carries the motto **"From Iwate to the World"** (岩手から世界へ). [source](https://furisake.com/projects/14) [source](https://greater-morioka-sc.jp/olympians_pjt/yachi-sora)
- **Partner / Relationship:** [DATA NOT FOUND]
- **Children:** [DATA NOT FOUND]
- **Education:** Attended **Morioka Chūō High School** (盛岡中央高校), Iwate Prefecture — a powerhouse for skiing. Graduated from **Waseda University**, Faculty of Sport Sciences (早稲田大学スポーツ科学部), majoring in coaching science. Described by Waseda as *"the successor to Watabe Akito"* (渡部暁の後継者). Currently employed by **JAL (Japan Airlines)** as an athlete employee since April 2023. [source](https://www.waseda.jp/inst/athletic/news/2022/02/08/24732/) [source](https://press.jal.co.jp/ja/release/202602/009262.html)
- **Off-Snow Interests:** Enjoys **reading**, **watching Korean dramas on Netflix**, **cycling**, and has a passion for **Indian curry**. [source](https://yachisora.com/2021/10/01/%E8%B0%B7%E5%9C%B0%E3%80%80%E5%AE%99/)
- **Notable Quotes:**
  - *"People can walk the life they envision."* — the quote that inspired him to pursue elite sport, attributed to Sangeida Reiichi [source](https://furisake.com/projects/14)
- **Personality / Public Persona:** Quiet determination embodied — a small-town kid from rural Iwate who was discovered through a talent identification program and methodically worked his way to the Olympic stage. Described as earnest and goal-oriented. Trained at Waseda alongside Watabe Akito and is seen as Watabe's successor in Japanese NC. His personal website emphasizes his *"from Iwate to the World"* journey. [source](https://yachisora.com/) [source](https://www.waseda.jp/inst/athletic/news/2022/01/21/23945/)

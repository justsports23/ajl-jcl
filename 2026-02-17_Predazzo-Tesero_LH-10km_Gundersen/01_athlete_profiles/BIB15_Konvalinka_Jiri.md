# Jiri Konvalinka (CZE)
**Bib:** 15 | **FIS Code:** 213443 | **DOB:** November 27, 2004 | **Age:** 21
**Club/Team:** DUKLA Liberec (youth: SK Harrachov) | **National Team Tier:** Czech Republic A-team

## Personal Bio
- **Hometown / Place of Origin:** Born and raised in **Harrachov**, Czech Republic — one of the country's most iconic ski jumping venues in the Krkonoše (Giant Mountains). Moved to **Liberec** for secondary school, joining **DUKLA Liberec** (the military sports club). [source](https://www.denik.cz/zimni_sporty/lyzovani-severska-kombinace-jiri-konvalinka-zacatek-sezony-ruka.html) [source](http://www.jirikonvalinka.cz/)
- **Family Background:** His **father was a Nordic Combined athlete** ("kombinák") — Jiří followed in his father's footsteps when he moved to Liberec. His younger **sister Linda Konvalinková** competes in **biathlon** for SKI Klub Harrachov and was recognized as one of the top young athletes of the Jablonec region in 2024. A true winter sports family from the heart of Czech skiing. [source](https://www.denik.cz/zimni_sporty/lyzovani-severska-kombinace-jiri-konvalinka-zacatek-sezony-ruka.html) [source](https://jablonecky.denik.cz/fotbal-kraj/vyhlaseni-nejlepsich-sportovcu-jablonecka-melo-hvezdne-viteze-i-hosty-20240209.html)
- **Childhood & Introduction to Skiing:** Started training in ski jumping and Nordic Combined **at age three** at **Jiskra Harrachov**. Has been competing in FIS events since 2016 (age 11-12). Won his first FIS Youth Cup race in January 2021 in Harrachov, where he and teammate Jan Šímek *"proved unbeatable"* in the Youth II Boys category. His role model is Norwegian dominant **Jarl Magnus Riiber**. [source](http://www.jirikonvalinka.cz/) [source](https://www.fis-ski.com/nordic-combined/news/2020-21/successful-fis-youth-cup-weekend-in-harrachov-cze)

- **Personality / Public Persona:** Quietly determined, level-headed, and genuinely enjoys the sport. Described as a *"man of light weight"* — an excellent jumper who is still developing his cross-country skiing. Despite being just 21, he has been competing internationally since age 11 and approached his Olympic debut with mature realism, setting a top-20 goal and achieving it. Two-time Czech "King of the White Trail" award winner. Won the **Alpen Cup Overall title** in 2023/24 with 7 race wins. German language fluency helped build relationships during training with Geiger, Rydzek, and other German world/Olympic champions. [source](https://www.czech-ski.com/severska-kombinace/aktuality/sport-si-moc-uzivam-a-bavi-me-popisuje-sdruzenar-jiri-konvalinka) [source](https://de.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ji%C5%99%C3%AD_Konvalinka)

- **World Cup wins/podiums:** WC career-best: **14th** (Ruka mass start, Nov 2025). [source](https://www.czech-ski.com/vsechny/aktualitycesi-zazarili-v-masaku-konvalinka-poprve-v-elitni-patnactce-vytrval-tesne-za-dvacitkou)

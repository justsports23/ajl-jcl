# Andreas Skoglund (NOR)
**Bib:** 27 | **FIS Code:** 189544 | **DOB:** March 22, 2001 | **Age:** 24
**Club/Team:** Molde og Omegn IF | **National Team Tier:** A-team

## Personal Bio
- **Hometown / Place of Origin:** From the Molde area, Norway (western Norway). Represents Molde og Omegn IF. [source](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Andreas_Skoglund) [source](https://www.fis-ski.com/DB/general/athlete-biography.html?sectorcode=nk&competitorid=189544&type=result)
- **Family Background:** Brother **Aleksander Skoglund** (born May 6, 1999) is also a competitive Nordic Combined skier on the international circuit. The brothers have competed together frequently — at the Continental Cup in Schonach, Andreas won with Aleksander on the podium in an all-Norwegian sweep. At Klingenthal, Andreas won by 28 seconds from Aleksander. [source](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Andreas_Skoglund) [source](https://www.nordicmag.info/en/nordic-combined-continental-cup-in-klingenthal-andreas-skoglund-wins-the-individual-compact-gael-blondeau-and-laurent-muhlethaler-in-the-top-10-0110/)
- **Childhood & Introduction to Skiing:** Grew up in western Norway. Made his World Cup debut at Holmenkollen in March 2019, placing 37th. Collected his first World Cup points in December 2019 in Lillehammer (28th and 16th). Won junior medals: bronze in team event (2018 Junior WCH), bronze in 10km individual and silver in team event (2019 Junior WCH). [source](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Andreas_Skoglund)

# Gasper Brecl (SLO)
**Bib:** 9 | **FIS Code:** 176006 | **DOB:** November 18, 1999 | **Age:** 26
**Club/Team:** SSK Mislinja (formerly SSK Velenje) | **National Team Tier:** Slovenia A-team

## Personal Bio
- **Hometown / Place of Origin:** Born and raised in **Velenje**, Slovenia. The city of Velenje celebrated having three athletes at the 2026 Olympics: Brecl, teammate Vid Vrhovnik, and snowboarder Gloria Kotnik. [source](https://www.velenje.si/trije-izjemni-sportniki-predstavljajo-velenje-na-zimskih-olimpijskih-igrah-gloria-kotnik-vid-vrhovnik-in-gasper-brecl/) [source](https://www.fis-ski.com/DB/general/athlete-biography.html?sectorcode=nk&competitorid=176006&type=result)
- **Family Background:** Sister **Jerneja Brecl** is a former competitive ski jumper who represented Slovenia internationally (including 2021 WCH). She now works as **External PR Officer and Social Media Manager** for the Slovenian Ski Association. Brother **Anže Brecl** is also involved in winter sports (ski jumping/NC). Sister **Viktorija Brecl** trained ski jumping briefly. The Brecl family is described as a true *"ski jumping family"* with at least three siblings actively competing. [source](https://siol.net/sportal/zimski-sporti/sportna-druzinica-ki-se-se-ni-soncila-v-medijskih-zarometih-599871)
- **Childhood & Introduction to Skiing:** Started with Nordic skiing (ski jumping) at **age 6** at SSK Velenje. Inspired by club predecessors **Marjan Jelenko** and **Gašper Berlot**, who proved good NC results were possible from Velenje. Switched club to SSK Mislinja in winter 2018/19. [source](https://siol.net/sportal/zimski-sporti/sportna-druzinica-ki-se-se-ni-soncila-v-medijskih-zarometih-599871) [source](https://www.koroski-radio.si/Lokalne-novice/ga%C5%A1per-brecl-%C4%8Dlan-ssk-mislinja-na-olimpijske-igre)
- **Partner / Relationship:** [DATA NOT FOUND]
- **Children:** [DATA NOT FOUND]
- **Education:** Attended **Gimnazija Franca Prešerna (GFP) in Kranj** — Slovenia's premier sports-focused high school, living in a dormitory alongside teammates Ema Volavšek and Silva Verbič. Completed his **undergraduate degree at the Faculty of Economics**. [source](https://siol.net/sportal/zimski-sporti/sportna-druzinica-ki-se-se-ni-soncila-v-medijskih-zarometih-599871)
- **Off-Snow Interests:** Academic focus — was working on completing his economics degree while competing internationally. [source](https://siol.net/sportal/zimski-sporti/sportna-druzinica-ki-se-se-ni-soncila-v-medijskih-zarometih-599871)
- **Notable Quotes:**
  - *"The jump was good. Already in training I showed that I can jump around the top twenty."* — after scoring his first WC points (28th, Ruka 2022) [source](https://siol.net/sportal/zimski-sporti/gasper-brecl-sezono-zacel-s-prvimi-tockami-v-karieri-593166)
  - *"I'm not satisfied, I expected more. It didn't go according to my plans. This hill doesn't suit me."* — after a disappointing home race at Planica WCH [source](https://siol.net/sportal/zimski-sporti/sportna-druzinica-ki-se-se-ni-soncila-v-medijskih-zarometih-599871)
- **Personality / Public Persona:** Part of the Slovenian NC *"športna družinica"* (sports family) — a tight-knit group of athletes who trained together from gymnasium days in Kranj. Described as athletes *"who are not yet accustomed to the media spotlight"* — humble, under-the-radar, hardworking. Combines athletic dedication with academic ambition. Personal best ski jump: **208 meters** at Planica (2022). Coach **Goran Janus** built the Slovenian NC A-team around four young athletes including Brecl and Vrhovnik. Current head coach: **Primož Triplat**. [source](https://siol.net/sportal/zimski-sporti/sportna-druzinica-ki-se-se-ni-soncila-v-medijskih-zarometih-599871) [source](https://www.fis-ski.com/nordic-combined/news/2019-20/goran-janus-takes-over-team-slovenia-marjan-jelenko-retires)

## Career Highlights
- **Olympic results:** First Olympics — **24th** NH/10km (MiCo 2026). 20th after jumping (93.5m, +1:15 behind leader), lost 4 positions in XC. [source](https://www.rtvslo.si/sport/oi-2026/sporti/nordijska-kombinacija/j-oftebro-olimpijski-prvak-v-nordijski-kombinaciji-brecl-24/773078)
- **World Championship results:** 3 WCH — best result **7th** (Mixed Team NH, Trondheim 2025, with Vrhovnik, Volavšek, Malovrh) [source](https://www.fis-ski.com/DB/general/athlete-biography.html?sectorcode=NK&competitorid=176006&type=result)
- **World Cup wins/podiums:** WC career-best: **28th** (Ruka, November 2022 — first WC points). [source](https://siol.net/sportal/zimski-sporti/gasper-brecl-sezono-zacel-s-prvimi-tockami-v-karieri-593166)
- **Mixed Team:** Part of Slovenia's **historic first-ever NC Mixed Team victory** at Tschagguns (2024, with Vrhovnik, Volavšek, Malovrh). [source](https://www.fis-ski.com/nordic-combined/news/2024-25/tschagguns-slovenia-writes-mixed-team-history)

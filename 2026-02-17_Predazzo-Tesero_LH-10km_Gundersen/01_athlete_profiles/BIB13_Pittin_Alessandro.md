# Alessandro Pittin (ITA)
**Bib:** 13 | **FIS Code:** 100009 | **DOB:** February 11, 1990 | **Age:** 36
**Club/Team:** G.S. Fiamme Gialle / Ski Club Aldo Moro Paluzza | **National Team Tier:** Italy A-team

## Personal Bio
- **Hometown / Place of Origin:** Born in **Tolmezzo**, Friuli Venezia Giulia, Italy. Represents Ski Club Aldo Moro Paluzza. [source](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alessandro_Pittin) [source](https://www.fis-ski.com/DB/general/athlete-biography.html?sectorcode=nk&competitorid=81371&type=result)
- **Family Background:** His **brother and father** were instrumental in getting him into the sport. Cousin **Cristina Pittin** made her Olympic debut in cross-country skiing at the 2022 Beijing Games. [source](https://www.fis-ski.com/nordic-combined/news/2018-19/articlealessandro-pittin)
- **Childhood & Introduction to Skiing:** Started skiing at **age three** and took up Nordic Combined at **age eight**. At **16**, he was the youngest participant in Nordic Combined at the 2006 Turin Olympics — his home Olympics. Won the **Junior World Championships in Zakopane 2008** at age 18. At **20**, he became the youngest Italian to win a Winter Olympic medal (bronze, Vancouver 2010). ## Career Highlights
- **Olympic results:** **6x Olympian** (2006, 2010, 2014, 2018, 2022, 2026). **Bronze NH/10km (Vancouver 2010)** — Italy's only individual Olympic medal in NC. 4th NH/10km (Sochi 2014). Youngest NC competitor at Turin 2006 (age 16). [source](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alessandro_Pittin)
- **World Championship results:** **9 WCH — Silver NH/10km (Falun 2015).** Junior WCH gold (Zakopane 2008). [source](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alessandro_Pittin)
- **World Cup wins/podiums:** **16 WC podiums — 3 wins, 4x 2nd, 9x 3rd.** First Italian WC winner (Chaux-Neuve, January 2012). [source](https://www.fis-ski.com/DB/general/athlete-biography.html?sectorcode=NK&competitorid=100009&type=result)

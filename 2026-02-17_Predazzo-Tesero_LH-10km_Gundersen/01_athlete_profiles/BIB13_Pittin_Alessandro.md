## Current Rankings
- World Cup Overall Standing: Outside top 15 — a veteran past his prime but still competitive [source](https://www.fis-ski.com/DB/nordic-combined/cup-standings.html)

## Career Highlights
- **Olympic results:** **6x Olympian** (2006, 2010, 2014, 2018, 2022, 2026). **Bronze NH/10km (Vancouver 2010)** — Italy's only individual Olympic medal in Nordic Combined [source](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alessandro_Pittin)
- **World Championship results:** **9 WCH — Silver NH/10km (Falun 2015)** [source](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alessandro_Pittin)
- **World Cup wins/podiums:** **16 WC podiums — 3 wins, 4x 2nd, 9x 3rd** [source](https://www.fis-ski.com)

## Technique & Format Profile
- **Jumping strength:** Good — consistent and experienced
- **Cross-country strength:** Strong — has shown elite cross-country speed throughout career
- **Sprint ability:** Good
- **Distance preference:** 10km Gundersen
- **Best format:** Individual Gundersen
- **Climbing ability:** Excellent — knows every meter of the Tesero course

## Key Storylines
- **6th Olympics at age 36** — ties with Watabe for the most Olympic appearances in this start list
- Competing in his **home valley of Val di Fiemme** — the most emotionally charged storyline of the entire event
- His 2010 Olympic bronze was won at age 20 — now 16 years later, he's still competing at the highest level
- This is almost certainly his **farewell Olympic race** in front of home fans
- Italy's all-time greatest Nordic Combined athlete — 3 WC wins, Olympic bronze, WCH silver
- The crowd support at the Predazzo ski jump and Tesero cross-country course will be extraordinary for him

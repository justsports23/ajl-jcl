## Career Highlights
- **Olympic results:** 4x Olympian (2014, 2018, 2022, 2026). Best: 5th NH/10km (MiCo 2026). At Sochi 2014, finished 14th and 16th at age 18. [source](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ilkka_Herola)
- **World Championship results:** 7 WCH — Silver NH/10km (Oberstdorf 2021) [source](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ilkka_Herola)
- **World Cup wins/podiums:** **21 WC podiums — 2 wins, 7x 2nd, 12x 3rd.** His first WC win in 2025 ended Finland's 15-year winless streak (since Hannu Manninen in 2009-10). [source](https://www.nordicmag.info/en/nordic-combined-lahti-ilkka-herola-breaks-the-curse-0321/)
- **Other major results:** Won team sprint with Hirvonen in Lahti 2019 (home crowd). Finland's most successful Nordic Combined athlete of his generation. [source](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ilkka_Herola)

## Technique & Format Profile
- **Jumping strength:** Average to good — not his strongest discipline
- **Cross-country strength:** **Elite** — one of the fastest skiers in the entire field; regularly overtakes athletes on the track
- **Sprint ability:** Very strong finishing kick
- **Distance preference:** 10km Gundersen
- **Best format:** Individual Gundersen — his cross-country speed allows him to chase down big deficits
- **Climbing ability:** Excellent; strong hill climber

## Key Storylines
- **5th in the NH/10km** — another "just missed" result for a career-long bridesmaid
- World Championship silver medalist (2021) — knows how to perform on the biggest stage
- At 30, this may be his **last Olympic Games** as Nordic Combined may not be in the 2030 program
- **Running for IOC Athletes' Commission** during these Games — dual role as athlete and advocate
- The large hill format creates **bigger gaps** from jumping — could challenge his ability to chase, but his XC speed is among the absolute best
- Part of a strong Finnish trio with Hirvonen (BIB 25, bronze medalist) and Karhumaa (BIB 10)
- A music-loving, drum-playing, climate-conscious athlete who has been the Finnish team leader since age 16

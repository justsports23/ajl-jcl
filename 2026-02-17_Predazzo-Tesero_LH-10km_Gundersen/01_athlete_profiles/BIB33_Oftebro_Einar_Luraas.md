# Einar Luras Oftebro (NOR)
**Bib:** 33 | **FIS Code:** 100553 | **DOB:** August 6, 1998 | **Age:** 27
**Club/Team:** IL Jardar | **National Team Tier:** A-team

## Personal Bio
- **Hometown / Place of Origin:** Høvik, Bærum, Norway (suburb west of Oslo). Full name: Einar Christian Lurås Oftebro. 5'8" (173 cm), 70 kg. Instagram: @eloftebro. [source](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Einar_Lur%C3%A5s_Oftebro) [source](https://networthmirror.com/einar-luras-oftebro/)
- **Family Background:** Older brother of Jens Luras Oftebro (BIB 35), the reigning NH/10km Olympic champion. The brothers are known as the "Oftebrothers" and have trained together throughout their careers. Their parents provided unwavering support — equipment, packed lunches, and transport across Norway to competitions. The Oftebro family maintains strong ties to their heritage, with active involvement in genealogical records and family gatherings (Lyngdal 2004, California 2010). [source](https://www.oftebro.com/2023-02-Nordic%20Combined.pdf) [source](https://www.nbcolympics.com/news/jens-luraas-oftebro-claims-norways-first-victory-nordic-combined-normal-hill-28-years)
- **Childhood & Introduction to Skiing:** Grew up in Høvik, Bærum. Both brothers became interested in combined skiing starting from their family cabin in Tinn, Telemark. Set the 29.5-meter Jardarkollen hill record in 2011 at age 13. Won silver in the 10km and bronze in the relay at Junior World Championships (2016-2018). [source](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Einar_Lur%C3%A5s_Oftebro) [source](https://www.oftebro.com/2023-02-Nordic%20Combined.pdf)
- **Partner / Relationship:** [DATA NOT FOUND] — the Oftebro brothers are private about personal matters
- **Children:** [DATA NOT FOUND]
- **Education:** Part-time engineering student (climate, environment and energy) at Oslo Metropolitan University (Oslo Met) alongside brother Jens. Training and competition are effectively their full-time job, with normal education put on hold. [source](https://www.oftebro.com/2023-02-Nordic%20Combined.pdf)
- **Off-Snow Interests:** The brothers are deeply engaged by their family heritage. Limited leisure time due to demanding competition schedule. [source](https://www.oftebro.com/2023-02-Nordic%20Combined.pdf)
- **Notable Quotes:**
  - *"It is just incredible. I was working so hard for this for many years but didn't get the results I felt I deserved. Now I am finally there and finally on top."* — after winning his maiden World Cup victory in Otepää, January 10, 2026 [source](https://www.fis-ski.com/nordic-combined/news/2025-26/einar-luraas-oftebro-takes-thrilling-maiden-world-cup-win-in-otepaeae)
  - *"I hope other athletes that struggle learn from this — never give up, trust your process and you will get there if you work hard enough and believe in yourself."* — inspirational message after Otepää WC win [source](https://www.fis-ski.com/nordic-combined/news/2025-26/einar-luraas-oftebro-takes-thrilling-maiden-world-cup-win-in-otepaeae)
  - *"We are privileged, we get to do what we like best."* — alongside brother Jens, on the demands of the NC lifestyle [source](https://www.oftebro.com/2023-02-Nordic%20Combined.pdf)
- **Personality / Public Persona:** The older but quieter of the "Oftebrothers." Spent years working in his younger brother's shadow before a dramatic breakout in 2025-26. His emotional reaction to his maiden WC win revealed years of perseverance. Described by NBC as "a stronger skier than ski jumper." [source](https://www.nbcolympics.com/news/jens-luraas-oftebro-claims-norways-first-victory-nordic-combined-normal-hill-28-years) [source](https://www.fis-ski.com/nordic-combined/news/2025-26/einar-luraas-oftebro-takes-thrilling-maiden-world-cup-win-in-otepaeae)

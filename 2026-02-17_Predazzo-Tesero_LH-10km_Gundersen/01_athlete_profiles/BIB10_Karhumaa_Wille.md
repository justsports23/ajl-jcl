# Wille Karhumaa (FIN)
**Bib:** 10 | **FIS Code:** 100495 | **DOB:** June 7, 2000 | **Age:** 25
**Club/Team:** Ounasvaaran Hiihtoseura (OH) | **National Team Tier:** Finland A-team

## Personal Bio
- **Hometown / Place of Origin:** Born in **Oulu**, Finland. Currently lives in **Rovaniemi**, Finland — the capital of Lapland. Speaks Finnish and English. [source](https://www.fis-ski.com/DB/general/athlete-biography.html?sectorcode=nk&competitorid=182985&type=result) [source](https://fi.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wille_Karhumaa)
- **Family Background:** Brother **Waltteri Karhumaa** also competed in Nordic Combined for Finland, representing Ounasvaaran Hiihtoseura. Waltteri competed at the 2023 Planica World Championships and scored his first WC points, but surprisingly withdrew from the national team at age 21. The Karhumaa brothers continued the legacy of **Hannu Manninen** (Olympic gold medalist) at their club after Manninen retired in 2018. Their coach was **Jukka Ylipulli**, a former elite NC athlete and Olympic medalist. [source](https://www.kaleva.fi/oulussa-syntynyt-wille-karhumaa-20-on-yhdistetyn-l/3354293) [source](https://www.lapinkansa.fi/waltteri-karhumaa-vetaytyi-maajoukkuetoiminnasta-i/5891969) [source](https://rukanordic.com/en/waltteri-karhumaa-continues-the-family-tradition-at-ruka-nordic/)
- **Childhood & Introduction to Skiing:** Started Nordic Combined at **kindergarten age** at Ounasvaaran Hiihtoseura in Rovaniemi. Has said that what fascinates him about NC is mastering two different sports: *"I'm never finished and there's always the possibility to surpass myself."* [source](https://yle.fi/a/3-10042441) [source](https://ulapland.fi/tarina/yhdistetyn-hiihtaja-wille-karhumaa-kun-muutin-takaisin-muistin-taas-kuinka-helppoa-pohjoisessa-on/)
- **Partner / Relationship:** [DATA NOT FOUND]
- **Children:** [DATA NOT FOUND]
- **Education:** Studying **law** (Master's degree program) at the **University of Lapland** in Rovaniemi. He chose Rovaniemi specifically because: *"Only here in the same city are the Faculty of Law and ski jumping hills."* Previously worked as a **sales representative at DNA Call Center** in Rovaniemi. Served as **Ambassador at the European Youth Olympic Games** for the Finnish Olympic Committee. [source](https://ulapland.fi/tarina/yhdistetyn-hiihtaja-wille-karhumaa-kun-muutin-takaisin-muistin-taas-kuinka-helppoa-pohjoisessa-on/) [source](https://sporteurope.org/eyoa-programme/wille-karhumaa-interview/)
- **Off-Snow Interests:** **Running and orienteering**, **tennis and frisbee golf**, and **law** (described as both a study and a passion). Favourite dish: **sautéed reindeer** (poronkäristys). Most inspiring athlete: **Petter Northug**. Secret superpower: *"A pretty good memory."* [source](https://sporteurope.org/eyoa-programme/wille-karhumaa-interview/) [source](https://www.fis-ski.com/DB/general/athlete-biography.html?sectorcode=nk&competitorid=182985&type=result)
- **Notable Quotes:**

  - *"When I moved back to Rovaniemi, I remembered again how easy life is in the north."* [source](https://ulapland.fi/tarina/yhdistetyn-hiihtaja-wille-karhumaa-kun-muutin-takaisin-muistin-taas-kuinka-helppoa-pohjoisessa-on/)
  - *"It was the worst jump of the whole trip. Definitely nerves. I pulled too far over the front. Annoying, but what can you do."* — after a disappointing Olympic ski jump [source](https://yle.fi/a/74-20209548/64-3-290342)
  - *"Life is short, enjoy your coffee."* — his life motto [source](https://sporteurope.org/eyoa-programme/wille-karhumaa-interview/)
- **Personality / Public Persona:** Ambitious and articulate, with clear goals stated publicly — wants to be among the world's best, not just top-20. Combines elite sport with serious **law studies** — intellectual and driven. Values friendship and community (chose "Friendship" as most important Olympic value). Down-to-earth Lapland personality — loves reindeer, coffee, and the simplicity of northern Finnish life. Carries the legacy of **Hannu Manninen** at Ounasvaaran Hiihtoseura with pride. [source](https://sporteurope.org/eyoa-programme/wille-karhumaa-interview/) [source](https://yle.fi/a/3-10042441)

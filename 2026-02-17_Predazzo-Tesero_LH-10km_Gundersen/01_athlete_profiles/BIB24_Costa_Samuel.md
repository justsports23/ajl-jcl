# Samuel Costa (ITA)
**Bib:** 24 | **FIS Code:** 100175 | **DOB:** November 30, 1992 | **Age:** 33
**Club/Team:** G.S. Fiamme Oro | **National Team Tier:** Italy A-team

## Personal Bio
- **Hometown / Place of Origin:** Born in Bolzano, Italy. Resides in **Selva di Val Gardena** (Wolkenstein in Gröden), South Tyrol. Nickname: **"Sam."** Competing on home soil — made his World Cup debut in Val di Fiemme in 2012. [source](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Samuel_Costa_(skier)) [source](https://www.fis-ski.com/en/nordic-combined/nordic-combined-news-multimedia/news-multimedia/news/archive/athlete-profiles/article=samuel-costa)
- **Family Background:** [DATA NOT FOUND]
- **Childhood & Introduction to Skiing:** Grew up in the Dolomites, Italy's Nordic skiing heartland. A talented ski jumper from a young age. Made his first international appearance at the 2011 WCH in Oslo (age 18, 47th). His first World Cup podium (3rd) came in **Seefeld in 2017**, and he also celebrated a Team Sprint podium at his home venue of Val di Fiemme. [source](https://www.fis-ski.com/en/nordic-combined/nordic-combined-news-multimedia/news-multimedia/news/archive/athlete-profiles/article=samuel-costa) [source](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Samuel_Costa_(skier))
- **Partner / Relationship:** Single [source](https://peoplepill.com/people/samuel-costa)
- **Children:** None [source](https://peoplepill.com/people/samuel-costa)
- **Education:** [DATA NOT FOUND] — spent time in the **USA** which changed his perspective on sports and life
- **Off-Snow Interests:** Has deliberately **stepped away from social media**: *"It is a relief not having to promote myself on Instagram. It simply takes too much time and energy to create content and share your daily life. My time and energy are so limited that I prefer to use it elsewhere."* [source](https://www.fis-ski.com/nordic-combined/news/2025-26/samuel-costa-the-passion-for-the-sport-is-still-the-same-)
- **Notable Quotes:**
  - *"The passion for the sport is still the same."* — on his comeback for a home Olympics after announcing retirement [source](https://www.fis-ski.com/nordic-combined/news/2025-26/samuel-costa-the-passion-for-the-sport-is-still-the-same-)
  - *"The Olympic Games were never really successful for me. It was a nice experience, but that's pretty much it."* — reflecting honestly on previous Olympic disappointments [source](https://www.fis-ski.com/nordic-combined/news/2025-26/samuel-costa-the-passion-for-the-sport-is-still-the-same-)
  - *"I was a completely different person back then. The sport was my identity, and for that reason, it was difficult to have a joyful life."* — on his personal growth and changed relationship with the sport [source](https://www.fis-ski.com/nordic-combined/news/2025-26/samuel-costa-the-passion-for-the-sport-is-still-the-same-)
  - *"The level of Nordic Combined has totally changed. The sport is getting more professional, and you need to do a lot of things in the right way to be successful."* — on the evolution of the discipline [source](https://www.fis-ski.com/nordic-combined/news/2025-26/samuel-costa-the-passion-for-the-sport-is-still-the-same-)
- **Personality / Public Persona:** A remarkably self-aware and candid athlete. Retired after the 2024 season but **came back for the home Olympics** in Val di Fiemme. Spent time in the USA which helped him realize *"the immense value that the Olympic Games hold."* Now competes with a healthier mindset — separating his identity from his sport. Anti-social-media in an era of athlete branding. At 33, the elder statesman of the Italian team alongside Pittin. [source](https://www.fis-ski.com/nordic-combined/news/2025-26/samuel-costa-the-passion-for-the-sport-is-still-the-same-)

## Career Highlights
- **Olympic results:** 3x Olympian (2014, 2022, 2026). Best individual: 30th (Sochi 2014). Best team: 8th Team LH/4x5km (Sochi 2014). [source](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Samuel_Costa_(skier))
- **World Championship results:** 7 WCH — 2x 4th (just off the podium, agonizingly close) [source](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Samuel_Costa_(skier))
- **World Cup wins/podiums:** **4 WC podiums — 4x 3rd.** First podium: 3rd in Seefeld (2017). [source](https://www.fis-ski.com/DB/general/athlete-biography.html?sectorcode=NK&competitorid=100175&type=result)
- **Other major results:** Came back from knee surgery at the start of 2015-16 season to finish 21st overall. Career-best WC result: 7th in Chaux-Neuve. [source](https://www.fis-ski.com/en/nordic-combined/nordic-combined-news-multimedia/news-multimedia/news/archive/athlete-profiles/article=samuel-costa)

## Key Storylines
- **Home nation athlete** — born in Bolzano, lives in Val Gardena, competing in Val di Fiemme — this is his backyard
- **Came out of retirement** specifically for the home Olympics — *"The passion for the sport is still the same"*
- His time in the **USA** transformed his relationship with the sport and life — a mature, self-aware competitor
- Anti-Instagram in an age of athlete branding — refreshingly authentic
- Part of Italy's 3-man team with Pittin (BIB 13) and Kostner (BIB 17)
- At 33, unlikely medal contender but the local crowd support could lift his performance
- Veteran of **7 World Championships and 3 Olympics** — brings invaluable experience to the Italian team

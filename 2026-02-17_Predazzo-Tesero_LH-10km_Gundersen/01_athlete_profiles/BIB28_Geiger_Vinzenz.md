- **Olympic results:** 3x Olympian. **Gold NH/10km (Beijing 2022)**, Gold Team LH (PyeongChang 2018), Silver Team LH (Beijing 2022) — **2 gold, 1 silver** [source](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vinzenz_Geiger)

- **World Cup wins/podiums:** **67 WC podiums — 22 wins, 23x 2nd, 22x 3rd.** Overall champion 2024-25. [source](https://www.fis-ski.com/DB/nordic-combined/cup-standings.html)
- **Other major results:** Debuted in World Cup in 2015-16 season. First WC win at Val di Fiemme in January 2019 [source](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vinzenz_Geiger)

## Key Storylines
- **Defending Olympic champion** in the NH/10km from Beijing 2022 — seeking to defend on the large hill

- **2024-25 World Cup overall champion** — has been the dominant force when healthy

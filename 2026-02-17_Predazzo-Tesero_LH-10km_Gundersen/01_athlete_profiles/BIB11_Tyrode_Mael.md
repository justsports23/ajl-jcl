# Mael Tyrode (FRA)
**Bib:** 11 | **FIS Code:** 194080 | **DOB:** July 31, 2000 | **Age:** 25
**Club/Team:** Ski Club Les Fourgs | **National Team Tier:** France B-team (elevated to A-team mid-season as 6th man)

## Personal Bio
- **Hometown / Place of Origin:** Born in **Pontarlier**, Doubs. Grew up near **Les Fourgs** in the **Jura Mountains**, Franche-Comté region — near the Swiss border, in the heart of French Nordic skiing territory. [source](https://www.fis-ski.com/DB/general/athlete-biography.html?sectorcode=nk&competitorid=194080&type=result) [source](https://sports.bourgognefranchecomte.fr/index.php/mael-tyrode-la-force-tranquille/)
- **Family Background:** *"Nordic Combined runs in his veins"* ("Le combiné nordique coule dans ses veines"), suggesting a family connection to the sport, though specific family details are not publicly documented. [source](https://sports.bourgognefranchecomte.fr/index.php/mael-tyrode-la-force-tranquille/)
- **Childhood & Introduction to Skiing:** Grew up at **Ski Club Les Fourgs** in the Jura massif — a traditional Nordic skiing region. Progressed through the French youth system with 4 Junior World Championship appearances (team silver medals in 2017 and 2020, 5th in 2018, 4th in 2019). Described as a *"mountain profile"* athlete, consistent with the terrain of his native Jura mountains. [source](https://www.equipedefrance.com/athlete/mael-tyrode) [source](https://sports.bourgognefranchecomte.fr/index.php/mael-tyrode-la-force-tranquille/)

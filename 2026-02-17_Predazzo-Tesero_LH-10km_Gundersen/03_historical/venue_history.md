# Venue History — Predazzo & Tesero, Val di Fiemme

## Overview

Val di Fiemme is one of the most storied Nordic skiing venues in the world. The Predazzo Ski Jumping Stadium ("Giuseppe Dal Ben" Arena) and the Tesero Cross-Country Skiing Stadium have hosted major international competitions since 1990.

## Nordic Combined World Cup at Val di Fiemme

The venue has hosted 21 Nordic Combined World Cup events in the following seasons:
1995, 1996, 1997, 1999, 2000, 2001, 2002, 2004, 2006, 2007, 2008, 2009, 2010, 2012, 2014, 2015, 2016, 2017, 2018, 2019, 2020.

[Source](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/%22Giuseppe_Dal_Ben%22_Ski_Jumping_Arena)

## Tour de Ski at Val di Fiemme

Val di Fiemme has hosted the final stages of the Tour de Ski since the tour's inception, featuring the iconic Final Climb up Alpe Cermis. While this is a cross-country skiing event (not Nordic Combined), it demonstrates the venue's importance to the Nordic skiing calendar and provides course familiarity data for combined athletes who also compete in cross-country World Cups.

[Source](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tour_de_Ski)
